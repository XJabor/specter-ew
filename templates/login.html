Specter EW is a browser-based tactical Electronic Warfare planning tool designed for EA/ES/EP mission analysis. Place enemy and friendly nodes on a live map to calculate jamming effectiveness (J/S margin), terrain-aware sensing ranges, and jammer coverage footprints. Supports directional antennas, frequency-hopping waveforms, clutter terrain types, and MGRS coordinate display. Built for use on a tactical LAN or hosted instance.
